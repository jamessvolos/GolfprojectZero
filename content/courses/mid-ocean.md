Mid Ocean Club is Macdonald's tropical masterpiece and the reason this map
reaches the mid-Atlantic. Built on the pink-sand coast of Bermuda, it carries
the full template vocabulary to an ocean setting, but it is remembered above
all for one hole.

The 5th, "Cape," is for many the finest Cape in the world — a par 4 bending
around a lagoon, its tee shot offering the player a pure diagonal gamble over
the water. Bite off as much of the carry as you dare and you are left a short
pitch from a good angle; play safe to the left and the hole stretches and the
angle sours. It is the Cape decision in its clearest, most beautiful form, and
it made the template famous far beyond the National.
