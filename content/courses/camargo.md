Camargo is the connoisseur's Raynor: a quiet inland course near Cincinnati
that many rank among the purest expressions of the Macdonald–Raynor template
school anywhere. Its reputation rests especially on its par 3s, a near-complete
set of the classic one-shot templates built to Raynor's bold, geometric
standard.

It earns its place here as the clearest single-course classroom for the
short-hole templates: you can walk from an Eden to a Short in an afternoon and
read the same designer posing two completely different questions on holes of
similar length. Camargo is proof that the template vocabulary, given a good
routing and firm ground, needs no ocean or ancient links to sing.
