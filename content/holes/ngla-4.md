The 4th at the National is Macdonald's definitive American Redan — the hole
through which the template entered the American game. It faithfully reproduces
the North Berwick original: a green set on a diagonal, tilting from front-right
to back-left, guarded short-left by a deep bunker, and inviting the running
shot fed off the right. Generations of architects studied this hole to learn
how the Redan works.
