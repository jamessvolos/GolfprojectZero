Fishers Island's Biarritz sets the long swale-green template against the open
coast, where the wind turns the choice between an all-carry aerial shot and a
lower running one through the trough into a genuine test of nerve. It is a
seaside cousin to Yale's monumental 9th and Chicago Golf Club's inland version
— the same idea, carried home from a lost French original and rebuilt, again,
somewhere new.
