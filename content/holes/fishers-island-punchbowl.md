Fishers Island's Punchbowl brings the friendliest of the templates to Raynor's
coastal masterpiece: a green cradled in a hollow, its surrounding slopes
collecting shots toward the centre. After the exposed, wind-blown holes around
it, the gather is a welcome gift — but position within the bowl still matters,
and the semi-blind approach still asks the player to commit to a line. It rounds
out Fishers Island's remarkable spread of templates.
