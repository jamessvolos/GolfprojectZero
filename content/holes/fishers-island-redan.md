Fishers Island's Redan carries the template to a wild coastal setting, where
Raynor's geometric shaping meets the wind and the water. It preserves the
essential invitation — the shot fed off the right that the slope delivers to a
hidden flag — while wearing the drama of its island ground. A useful
counterpoint to the gentler original at North Berwick and the amplified version
at Yale.
