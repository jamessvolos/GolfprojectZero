The 4th at Yale is the Redan given the same monumental treatment as the rest of
the course — a long one-shotter to a green set on the classic diagonal, tilted
away from the player and guarded short-left by a deep bunker, inviting the
running shot fed off the right. It faithfully preserves the Redan decision
while wearing Yale's characteristically bold, oversized shaping, a useful
counterpoint to the gentler original on the North Berwick links.
